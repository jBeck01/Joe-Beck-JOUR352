Lottery retailer customers are mostly local, analysis shows

By Aadit Tambe, Michael Purdie, Shreya Vuttaluru, Jamie Pinzon and Rachel Logan

The Howard Center analyzed mobile traffic location data from SafeGraph, a location intelligence firm that collects information about store foot traffic. The first-of-its-kind analysis, which examined store traffic patterns at three-quarters of all US lottery retailers, found that a majority of lottery retailers had a customer base that included more than 50 percent of visitors living in local neighborhoods, in all but two states.
