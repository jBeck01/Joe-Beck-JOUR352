2020-2021 Maryland Mens Basketball Stats

Reflection on the Terrapins past basketball season

These graphs shown below will display the statistics of the top ten players on the Maryland Mens Basketball team from the 2020-2021 season. Fatts Russell, Eric Ayala, Donta Scott, Hakim Hart, Qudus Wahab, Julian Reese, Ian Martinez, Xavier Green, Marcus Dockery and Simon Wright were the top 10 players from that season, listed respectively to their rank.
